What is BMI?

BMI stands for "body mass index" and is a way to meassure the weight of ones body compared to the length. BMI aims to determine the amount of body fat for a person. Having a too high or too low percentage of body fat may lead to heigthened risk of a number of serious diseases.

With this simple tool, you can calculate your own Body Mass Index, which might be a good indicator for your current amount of body fat. The table below will help explain what your BMI means.
